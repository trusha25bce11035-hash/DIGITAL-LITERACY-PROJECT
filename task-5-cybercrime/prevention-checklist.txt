Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist 
Prevention Checklist for College Students

 
Stay Safe Online 

➢ Before clicking on any link, you receive via WhatsApp, 
Instagram or email, confirm that the link goes where it says 
it goes – site411 will show you multiple sources. 
➢ You should never give anyone your OTP or passwords, 
even if they say they work at your bank or other legitimate 
establishment.  
➢ Never give out any personal information, including your 
bank information, to anyone. 
➢ Use strong and unique passwords for all your different 
accounts and change them often.  
➢ Always enable 2FA on your accounts for additional 
security benefits. 
➢ Avoid using public Wi-Fi to log into any banking 
application and make any payments. 
➢ Be careful about messages that create an immediate sense 
of urgency like “Pay Now!” or “Block Your Account” 
➢ Make sure that your applications and phone software are up 
to date, to help prevent any security breaches of your 
device or loss of data. 


UPI & Financial Safety 

➢ Prior to giving approval for any UPI transactions, always 
confirm with the receiver that their name is the same as 
what you were given. 
➢ Only approve "collect requests" from a sender you know 
with complete confidence that they really sent it to you. 
➢ Do not scan random QR Codes sent to you from unknown 
sources. 


Report Cybercrime 

if you ever face or suspect online fraud, report immediately: 
• National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
