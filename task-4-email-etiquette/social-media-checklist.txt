Part B – Social Media Do’s and Don’ts 
Checklist 

Social Media Checklist for College Students  


The Do’s 

➢ Think carefully before you post something on the media. 
You want to make sure that what you post is what you want 
people to think of you. 
➢ You can use media to show people what you are like, 
especially on sites like LinkedIn that are used for business. 
➢ Always check to make sure what you are sharing is true. 
This will help stop people from sharing information. 
➢ Be nice and respectful when you talk to people in the 
media. You can comment on their posts. Send them private 
messages. 
➢ You can use the privacy settings on your profile to control 
who can see what you post.

 
The Don’ts 

➢ Do not post anything that could be seen as offensive or 
hurtful to others. 
➢ Do not share much about yourself on social media like 
where you live or your phone number. 
➢ Try not to say things about your university or the people 
who work there. 
➢ Do not get into arguments with people, on media that are 
not necessary. 
➢ Do not post things without checking if they are true like 
news or funny pictures. You should always verify media 
posts, especially social media news and social media 
information, to make sure social media is used responsibly 
and that you are sharing accurate social media content.
