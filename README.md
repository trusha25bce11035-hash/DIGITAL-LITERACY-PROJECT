## Project Overview
The Student Digital Ambassador Program was enacted in order to create a project with the objective of enhancing essential skill sets for the practical application of safe and effective use of technology and the internet.  The project is broken up into five tasks, each of which covers an area of importance including: Digital Literacy, Professional Identity, Communication Etiquette, and Cybercrime Awareness. 
Digital ambassadors will use tools like: Canva, GitHub, and HackerRank to demonstrate how to build a positive digital footprint. The project consists of a case study designed to educate digital ambassadors and their respective communities about different types of cybercrime (phishing) and the various dangers associated with being online. At the end of the project participants will be equipped with the necessary knowledge and skill set to become responsible digital citizens and feel confident while acting as digital citizens.
